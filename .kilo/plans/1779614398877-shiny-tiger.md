## Phase 0 â€” Decisions (confirmed)

### Q1: Session management pattern â†’ **Option A (library-only)**
`events.py` and `hooks.py` accept an `AsyncSession` parameter. No `get_db()` or `init_db()` in the library. The consuming app owns session lifecycle. Thin re-exports of `engine` and `AsyncSessionLocal` may be exposed in `engram/__init__.py` for convenience without context-manager wrappers.

### Q2: `compact_entries()` commit strategy â†’ **flush-only internally**
`_add_memory_entry(_internal)` uses `add()` + `flush()` only. `compact_entries` and `create_snapshot` own the single `commit()` + `refresh()` on the outer session.

### Q3: Python / pytest minimums â†’ **Python 3.11+, pytest-asyncio>=0.21**
